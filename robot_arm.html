How to Control

Press the following buttons with shift to do the reverse

  • S/s: Rotate shoulder
  • E/e: Rotate elbow
  • F/f: Rotate fingers
  • X/x: Rotate view around X-axis
  • Y/y: Rotate view around Y-axis
  • A/a: Rotate fingers around X-axis
  • B/b: Rotate fingers around X-axis (opposite)
  • M/m: Rotate fingers around Y-axis
  • N/n: Rotate fingers around Y-axis (opposite)
  • T: Toggle wire/solid cube
  • P: Toggle projection type
